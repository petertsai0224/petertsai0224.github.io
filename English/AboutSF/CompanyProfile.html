Company profile

Sunny Friend Environmental Technology co., ltd. is the first professional bio-medical waste incineration plant in Taiwan. The company has the most advanced leading technologies. The company has successfully employed the technologies imported from abroad to the handling of all types of hazardous waste at home and abroad. In the meantime, the company also engages in collection, incineration, solidification, physicochemical and landfill of hazardous waste and it has effectively integrated a stream-line one-stop professional service from upper, middle and lower reaches.

Year of Establishment: 1994
Chairman of the Board and General Manager:
Chang Fang-Cheng
Capital: Paid-in capital 1.115 billion NT
Number of employees: 407 (as of 2016/12/31)
Company Address: No.1-20, Yuan Dong Rd., Yuanchang Township, Yunlin County

Company History

Sunny Friend Environmental Technology co., ltd. was established in 1994. In 2001, Sunny Friend Environmental Technology co., ltd. became the base of the Environmental Protection Industry for the Ruentex Group and also the first professional bio-medical waste incineration plant in Taiwan. Sunny Friend Environmental Technology co., ltd. accumulated more than 20 years of experience in engineering and operations management and mastered a full range of technologies in R&D, design, operations, system improvements, etc. Sunny Friend Environmental Technology co., ltd. has successfully improved and strengthened the Environmental Protection Industry in Taiwan.

  • 1994  November   Sunny Friend Waste Disposal co., ltd. was established. Paid-in capital was 50 million NT.
  • 1997  January   The Yunlin County Government issued the Class A incineration plant operations license.
  • 2000  July   company was renamed as Sunny Friend Environmental Technology co., ltd.
  • 2000  December   The company passed ISO14001 environment system certification from the NSF review.
  • 2001  December   General Manager, Mr. Chang Fang-Cheng, took office.
  • 2002  May   General Manager, Mr. Chang Fang-Cheng, took office.Obtained the Operations Permit issued by the county government. Yunlin second plant began operations.
  • 2005  June   Investment in the establishment of Beijing Ruentex Environmental Protection Technology Corp. in mainland China.
  • 2007  June   Chairman of the board, Mr. Chang Fang-Cheng, took office.
  • 2012  February   The Medical waste incineration plant built for Beijing Ruentex Environment Technology Corp. was completed.
  • 2012  November   Obtain the right to operate the Central District Industrial Waste Integrated Processing Center (originally managed by a Veteran Company, which is Zhangbin plant.
  • 2012  December   Paid-in Capital increased to 1 billion NT.
  • 2013  December   Planning to set up a salary compensation committee
  • 2014  March   OTC Login
  • 2015  March   Public Listing
  • 2015  March   IPO & Paid-in Capital increased to 1.115 billion NT.
